Frequently Asked Questions

How do I vote in the upcoming elections?

To vote in the elections:

  1. Log in to the voting system using your student credentials
  2. Navigate to the "Current Elections" section
  3. Select each position and choose your preferred candidate
  4. Review your selections and submit your vote
  5. You'll receive a confirmation receipt with your voting details
Is my vote anonymous and secure?

Yes, our voting system uses blockchain technology to ensure:

  • Complete anonymity - your selections cannot be linked to your identity
  • Tamper-proof security - votes cannot be altered once cast
  • Verifiability - you can confirm your vote was counted without revealing your choice

Your student credentials are only used to verify eligibility, not to track your votes.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues while voting?

If you experience technical difficulties:

  1. Refresh the page and try again
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended)
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  5. If issues persist, contact our support team immediately

Note: The system will preserve your selections if you need to refresh or restart.

Can I change my vote after submitting?

No, for security and integrity reasons, votes cannot be changed once submitted. This ensures:

  • Prevention of vote selling or coercion
  • Maintenance of election integrity
  • Consistency with physical voting procedures

You can review your selections carefully before final submission.

How can I verify that my vote was counted?

After voting, you'll receive:

  • A confirmation receipt with a unique transaction ID
  • The option to print or save your receipt
  • Access to view your voting history (without revealing your selections)

Your transaction ID can be used to verify your vote was included in the final count while maintaining anonymity.

Troubleshooting Guide

I can't log in to the voting system

If you're having trouble logging in:

  1. Ensure you're using your correct student ID and password
  2. Check that your account is active (not suspended or graduated)
  3. Try resetting your password if you've forgotten it
  4. Make sure you're trying to log in during the official voting period
  5. Contact IT support if you suspect your account has issues
The page is loading slowly or not displaying properly

For performance or display issues:

  • Check your internet connection - switch to a more stable network if possible
  • Update your browser - use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  • Clear cache and cookies - this often resolves display issues
  • Try a different device - switch to a computer if using a phone, or vice versa
  • Disable browser extensions - some ad blockers or privacy tools may interfere
I'm getting an error message when trying to vote

Common error messages and solutions:

Error Solution
"Voting period has not started" Check the election dates and try again during the official voting period
"You have already voted" Each student can only vote once per election
"Required fields not completed" Ensure you've selected candidates for all required positions
"System timeout" Refresh the page and start again (your progress may be saved)

For other errors, note the exact message and contact support.

I didn't receive my voting receipt

If you don't receive your receipt after voting:

  1. Check your spam/junk folder in your email
  2. Verify you provided the correct email address
  3. Look in your voting history - the receipt may be available there
  4. Wait a few minutes - sometimes there's a slight delay
  5. If still missing, contact support with your voting details

Note: You can always print/save the receipt displayed immediately after voting.
